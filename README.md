#telegram list

List of interesting groups, channels and telegram bots. Telegram chats for programmers and developers, geeks, IT people, sysadmins and devops, as well as other people who are not indifferent to computers and programming.

Colleagues, if you have interesting channels, chats or bots that are interesting for programmers, coders and developers, and relevant to interesting IT topics - please send your suggestions and pull requests. We will be happy to expand our useful list for everyone.
Please note that we only add relevant resources!

* [Telegram Group List](https://t.me/listnews) - The official news channel of this group list
